Privacy Policy

Welcome to AmdavadStay. We value your trust and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you visit our platform to find PG accommodations in Ahmedabad.

1. Information We Collect

When you use AmdavadStay, book a PG room, or fill out forms on our website, we may collect the following information:

  • Personal Details: Name, Email Address, Contact Number, and Gender.
  • Booking Information: Preferred PG location, sharing type, monthly budget, and check-in date.
  • Payment Details: UPI ID or partial card metadata processed through our secure validation window (we never store complete payment pins or passwords).

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is strictly utilized to deliver and improve our services:

  • To verify your PG bookings and process your payments safely.
  • To connect you seamlessly with the verified PG owners in Ahmedabad.
  • To respond to your support queries submitted via our Contact system.

3. Data Security

Your data security is our top priority. We use secure servers and standard encryption frameworks to keep your personal records hidden from unauthorized access. Our interactive payment validation ensures your payment workflows remain fully secure.

4. Cookies Policy

We use essential cookies to maintain your active login sessions and remember temporary configurations like your selected PG matches or your local booking cart contents.
